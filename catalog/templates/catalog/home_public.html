How It Works

OCKA provides a simple and secure wholesale ordering process built for both retailers and wholesalers.

  1. Browse & Order

    Step 1

    Retailers browse products, add items to cart, and book orders with no upfront payment.

  2. Order Review

    Step 2

    Wholesaler reviews the order, adds shipping and GST, and confirms it, sending a payment request.

  3. Secure Payment

    Step 3

    Retailer receives the final invoice and makes a secure payment through the platform.

  4. Track Shipment

    Step 4

    Once payment is confirmed, the wholesaler ships the order. Retailers can track progress to their doorstep.

Funds are held safely until shipment, ensuring security for both parties.
